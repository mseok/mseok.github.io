About Me

Hi, I am a graduate student at KAIST Chemistry, advised by Prof. Woo Youn Kim. I am interested computer-aided drug discovery, especially in deep learning-based protein-ligand interaction prediction and generative modeling of small molecules for drug candidates. Starting with PIGNet, a physics-informed deep learning model for protein-ligand interaction prediction, my current work focuses on developing a versatile model for protein-ligand interactions in an end-to-end data-driven manner.
